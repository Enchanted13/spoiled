How Spoiled Works

Spoiled looks on every webpage to see if any of the Blocked Phrases you provide exist on that page.

  • All of the Blocked Phrase must match, including spaces
  • Blocked Phrases can be any case

For example, the phrase "Star Wars"

  • Blocks "Star Wars: the Force Awakens" (Star Wars matches)
  • Blocks "STAR WARS" (case does not matter)
  • Does not block "StarWars" (spaces affect matching)
  • Does not block "Star Trek" (all words in the phrase must be found)
